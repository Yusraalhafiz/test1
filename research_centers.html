Research Centers at UPSI

Discover the various research centers at UPSI.

The Pusat Penyelidikan Perkembangan Kanak-Kanak Negara (National Child Development Research Centre) at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a premier institution dedicated to advancing the study of child development. This center focuses on comprehensive research related to the physical, cognitive, emotional, and social development of children. It aims to provide valuable insights and evidence-based practices that can inform educators, policymakers, and parents. Equipped with modern facilities and staffed by expert researchers, the center conducts various programs, workshops, and seminars to promote the well-being and optimal development of children in Malaysia and beyond.
