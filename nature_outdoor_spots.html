Nature & Outdoor Spots at UPSI

Explore the beautiful nature and outdoor spots at UPSI.

Tasik Proton at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a picturesque lake situated within the campus, offering a tranquil environment for relaxation and recreation. The lake is a popular spot for students and staff to unwind, featuring well-maintained walking paths, benches, and shaded areas for picnics and quiet study sessions. It is also the site for various outdoor activities such as kayaking, paddle boating, and small-scale events that enhance campus life. Tasik Proton not only provides a natural retreat from academic life but also supports a sense of community and well-being among the UPSI community.
Sungai Teratak at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a serene and scenic river area within the campus, providing a natural retreat for students and staff. Surrounded by lush greenery, it serves as a perfect spot for relaxation, picnics, and outdoor study sessions. The tranquil setting of Sungai Teratak also makes it an ideal location for nature walks, photography, and small gatherings. This peaceful environment not only offers a break from academic life but also encourages a connection with nature, promoting overall well-being within the UPSI community.
