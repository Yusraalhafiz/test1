Sports & Recreation at UPSI

Engage in various sports and recreational activities at UPSI.

Bowling at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) offers a fun and engaging recreational activity for students and staff. The bowling facilities are designed to provide a relaxing environment where everyone can enjoy a friendly game or participate in competitive tournaments. It's a great way to unwind, socialize, and build camaraderie within the university community. Whether you're a seasoned bowler or a beginner, the bowling experience at UPSI promises enjoyment and an opportunity to strike up some memorable moments.
The Arena at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a state-of-the-art facility designed to host a wide range of sports and recreational activities. Equipped with modern amenities, the arena features indoor courts for basketball, badminton, and volleyball, as well as a gymnasium and fitness center. It serves as a hub for students and staff to engage in physical activities, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. The Arena also hosts various sports competitions, events, and fitness programs, fostering a spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship within the UPSI community.
The swimming pool at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a modern and well-maintained facility that provides an excellent environment for both recreational and competitive swimming. Open to students, staff, and the university community, the pool offers dedicated lanes for lap swimming, as well as areas for leisure and relaxation. It is an ideal place for swimming lessons, aquatic fitness classes, and swim meets. The facility ensures a safe and enjoyable experience with professional lifeguards on duty and clean, well-kept amenities. The swimming pool at UPSI promotes health, wellness, and a love for aquatic sports among its users..
Kayaking at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) offers students and staff an exciting way to engage with the great outdoors. Located on the campus's serene lake, the kayaking facility provides all necessary equipment and safety gear for an enjoyable experience. Whether for leisure paddling or as part of an organized sports activity, kayaking at UPSI allows participants to build physical fitness, teamwork, and a sense of adventure. The calm waters and scenic surroundings make it a perfect setting for both beginners and experienced kayakers to explore and appreciate nature.
