Events & Festivals at UPSI

Stay updated with the latest events and festivals at UPSI.

The Pesta Buku Festival at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a highly anticipated annual event that celebrates the joy of reading and the world of literature. The festival brings together authors, publishers, and book enthusiasts for a series of book launches, readings, and discussions. Alongside these literary activities, there are also numerous stalls selling a wide range of books, from academic texts to popular novels. The event provides a wonderful opportunity for the UPSI community and the public to explore new books, meet authors, and engage in enriching conversations about literature and learning.
The Peskon Festival at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a dynamic event that highlights innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among students. Peskon, short for "Pesta Konvokesyen," features a variety of activities, including tech exhibitions, business pitch competitions, workshops, and networking sessions. It aims to inspire and empower students to turn their ideas into reality and to showcase their entrepreneurial talents. The festival creates a vibrant atmosphere on campus, fostering collaboration and innovation while celebrating the entrepreneurial spirit within the UPSI community.
The Vendor Food Festival at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI) is a delightful annual event that brings together a diverse array of food vendors, offering a wide range of delicious cuisines. Held on the university grounds, the festival features local and international dishes, tempting food enthusiasts with a variety of flavors and culinary experiences. In addition to the delectable food, the festival includes cooking demonstrations, live music, and cultural performances, creating a festive and lively atmosphere. The Vendor Food Festival provides a wonderful opportunity for the UPSI community and visitors to enjoy great food, socialize, and experience the rich culinary traditions in a vibrant setting.
