Outdoor Activities at UPSI

Discover the various outdoor activities you can enjoy at UPSI.

UPSI Adventure Park in Tanjong Malim offers a variety of exciting outdoor activities for students and visitors. The park features thrilling options like rock climbing, jungle trekking, and obstacle courses that challenge both physical endurance and teamwork. It's an ideal spot for adventure enthusiasts to engage in recreational activities while enjoying the natural beauty of the surroundings. Whether you're looking for a fun day out or a team-building experience, UPSI Adventure Park has something for everyone.
At UPSI Adventure Park, the ATV rides provide an exhilarating experience for adventure seekers. Riders can navigate through diverse terrains, from rugged paths to forest trails, all while enjoying the scenic beauty of the park. The ATV rides offer a perfect blend of excitement and outdoor exploration, making it a popular activity for students and visitors looking for a thrilling adventure. Whether you're a novice or an experienced rider, the ATV experience at UPSI Adventure Park is sure to leave you with lasting memories.
